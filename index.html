Breed Overview

Angus cattle are one of the most widely used beef breeds in the world. Known for their high-quality marbled beef, they are naturally hornless and highly adaptable.

Current Name: Angus

Previous Names: Aberdeen Angus, Polled Scotch

Why Angus?

  • Excellent beef quality
  • Strong genetics
  • Efficient growth
  • Calm temperament
