Why Angus dominates beef production

Purpose

Angus cattle are raised primarily for beef production. Their meat is known for tenderness, flavor, and marbling.

Strengths

  • High carcass quality
  • Efficient feed conversion
  • Polled genetics
  • Strong market demand

Weaknesses

  • Lower heat tolerance
  • Dark coats absorb heat
  • Requires genetic management
